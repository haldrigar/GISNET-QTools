    ---------------------------------------------

    GISNET QTools is a plugin designed to facilitate data retrieval from Polish government portals.

    Its goal is to enable users to quickly search for data of interest and redirect them to the appropriate external portal by clicking on the map.

    The plugin utilizes the OBLIVIEW portal from MGGP Aero Sp. z o.o. (https://obliview.com).

    This is a preliminary version of the plugin, which will be expanded with additional features.

    The plugin uses icons generated by Flaticon.com
