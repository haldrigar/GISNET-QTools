about=GISNET QTools is a plugin designed to facilitate data acquisition from Polish government portals.
  
    Its purpose is to help users quickly retrieve the data they are interested in and redirect them to the appropriate external portal by clicking on the map.
  
    This is an initial version of the plugin, which will be expanded with additional features.
  
    The first functionality is redirecting from the map to the oblique imagery portal for the city of Gdańsk or Gdynia
